How to subscribe

Google Google Calendar

  1. Open Google Calendar
  2. Click the + next to "Other calendars" → From URL
  3. Paste your subscription URL and click Add calendar

Outlook Outlook / Microsoft 365

  1. Open Outlook Calendar → Add calendar
  2. Choose Subscribe from web
  3. Paste your subscription URL and click Import

Apple Apple Calendar (Mac / iPhone)

  1. Open Calendar → File → New Calendar Subscription
  2. Paste your subscription URL and click Subscribe
  3. Set auto-refresh to Every day
