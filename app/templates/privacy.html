Credanta / Privacy

Privacy Policy

Credanta is built to help professionals manage sensitive career documents. This page explains what we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your information.

What Credanta Does

Credanta is a document management platform for professionals. Users may upload resumes, certifications, professional licenses, identification documents, onboarding records, and related career materials. These documents are private to each user's account by default.

Information We Collect

Credanta only collects information required to operate the service:

  • Account information — your name and email address, provided when you sign in with Google.
  • Uploaded documents — files and metadata (filename, category, expiration date) that you provide directly.
  • Usage data — basic activity logs used to improve the platform and debug issues.

We do not collect payment card details directly. If payment processing is enabled, it is handled by a third-party provider (Stripe) subject to their own privacy policy.

How Your Documents Are Stored

Uploaded documents are stored privately and are only accessible to you by default. Documents are not indexed, shared with third parties, or used for any purpose other than operating your account.

If you create a share link, selected documents may be accessible to the recipient of that link. You control whether to create, limit, or revoke share links from within your account.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your documents or personal data.
  • We do not use uploaded documents for advertising or profiling.
  • We do not share your documents with employers, agencies, or third parties unless you initiate a share link.
  • We do not make documents publicly searchable.

Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at support@credantaapp.com. Upon verified request, we will delete your account, uploaded files, and associated metadata within a reasonable timeframe.

Third-Party Services

Credanta uses Google OAuth for authentication. Your sign-in is governed by Google's own privacy policy. Credanta only receives your name and email address from Google at the time of sign-in.

Contact

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns, contact us at support@credantaapp.com.

Note: This page is a general privacy summary intended for transparency. It should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before full public launch to ensure compliance with applicable privacy laws (such as GDPR, CCPA, or other regional regulations).
