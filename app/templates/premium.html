Common questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — you can downgrade or cancel your plan at any time. Your documents and data are always yours.

Is my data secure?

All documents are stored encrypted and are only accessible to you (and recruiter links you explicitly create). We never share your data.

What happens when a credential expires?

Premium users get email reminders 30 and 7 days before expiry. Expired credentials are flagged on your dashboard so nothing slips through.

What is a share link?

A Premium+ share link gives recruiters a read-only view of your current credential packet — they can download individual files or the whole zip without needing an account.

How does AI parsing work?

When you upload a document, Credanta uses OCR and AI to automatically detect the credential category and expiration date, saving you from typing it manually.

Is there a file size limit?

Each file can be up to 25 MB. That's plenty for high-quality scans of licenses, certifications, and ID documents.
