PRIVACY POLICY PAGE
────────────────────────

Title:
Privacy Policy

Content should clearly explain:

- Credanta helps users store and manage professional documents.
- Users may upload resumes, certifications, licenses, IDs, onboarding records, and related professional documents.
- Credanta only collects information needed to operate the service.
- Uploaded documents are private to the user unless the user creates a share link.
- Credanta does not sell user documents.
- Credanta does not use uploaded documents for advertising.
- Users can request account deletion.
- Users can contact Credanta for privacy questions.

Add disclaimer:
This page is a general privacy summary and should be reviewed by legal counsel before full public launch.

HOW YOUR DOCUMENTS ARE SECURED PAGE
────────────────────────

Title:
How Your Documents Are Secured

Explain the document security workflow:

1. User uploads a document
2. Document is stored in private storage
3. Metadata is used to organize and monitor the document
4. Access is limited to the account owner
5. Share links are temporary and controlled by the user
6. Users can revoke share links where supported

Include clear trust language:
- Your documents are not public by default.
- Share links should only expose selected documents.
- Expired links should no longer allow access.
- Sensitive files should never be indexed publicly.

Add note:
Do not upload patient records, protected health information, or documents you are not authorized to store.
